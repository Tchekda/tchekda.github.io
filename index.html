What am I doing else

What I'm doing for others and our society

Outside of my studies and sports activities, I also like to volunteer on my freetime, in areas I am passionate about. It allows me to try new things in a friendly environment, without any professional expectations.

Being a self-taught developer, I wished so many times to have someone to help me and guide me in my learning process, so I decided to give back to the community by helping students in their learning process. I started by leading workshops at CoderDojo Paris, and later join Prologin / GirlsCanCode (national organization)

I have developed a passion for aviation which led me to buy a Flight Simulation software and join an online network, IVAO.aero. One thing led to another and I ended up joining the organization's Web Dev team, after some years I took over the leadership of the team and now I am in charge of a team of 10 developers, working on the various projects. Applying my tech skills to the aviation world is an awesome opportunity.

In 2024, Paris hosted the Summer Olympics & Paralympics; since I didn't only want to spectate and wanted to contribute as I could to the event, I applied to be a volunteer. I requested to be placed at a leadership position and was assigned to co-lead the Olympic & Paralympic Family Assistant teams (300 volunteers each). I quickly proved myself valuable and trustworthy within the organization, and was given additional responsibilities to support the operations and ensure smooth coordination between the various parties attending the event.
